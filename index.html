About Us

At FresherTalk Digital Solutions, we are committed to bridging the gap between academic learning and industry requirements by equipping fresh graduates and aspiring IT professionals with cutting-edge technical skills and hands-on experience. Our mission is to ensure that students are job-ready with industry-standard knowledge, practical exposure, and placement support.

Recognized under the MSME Udyam scheme of the Government of India and the Uttar Pradesh Shops and Commercial Establishment Act, 1962, we specialize in IT training, internships, and career development programs tailored for B.Tech, MCA, BCA, and IT students. Our AICTE-recognized internships provide students with real-world exposure in Web Development, Java, Cloud Computing, and more, ensuring they meet the demands of the IT industry.
